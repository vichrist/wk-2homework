The potential of personal and professional growth is a privilege of opportunity, and opportunity is what allows us to discover both our interests and passions. Moreso frequently I find that educating myself has become quite ordinary, the idea of learning and exerting that knowledge to serve as a resource of any sort is a reward of its own.

While I have embarked on a few different journeys and have accomplished those endeavors, I admit that I have not continued to seek out those interests, rather I have taken on a new journey and have finally recognized that my interests and passions have led me to different paths. For the very first time I have felt a passion for what I am learning and what I will eventually be doing-----Full Stack Web Development.

I am cognizant that I still have a long journey ahead and quite a bit to learn, it is indeed the in-depth challenges of Web Development that has sparked my interest and I am looking forward to mastering what I have learned thus far as well as taking on new challenges in whole. Taking an active approach to learn something new has always been a part of who I am, but this time it really is different-- I am pursuing a passion rather than an interest and that has made me feel complete within.

If I had to describe one thing that I cherish in life, it is the time that we are given. In essence all we have is time along with choices---may as well be wise with them both!
